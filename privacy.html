Privacy Statement

Applies to https://sounny.github.io and all related subdomains.

Our commitment

Your privacy matters. This statement explains how information is handled when you visit sounny.github.io and any subdomains hosted under this site (collectively, the “Services”). The Services are intended to operate in full compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the ePrivacy Directive, and applicable US federal and state privacy regulations.

What we collect

No personal data is intentionally collected, stored, or shared through the Services. The site does not use cookies, analytics tags, advertising trackers, or other mechanisms to profile visitors.

Server and network logs

Our hosting provider may generate minimal technical logs (for example, IP address, browser type, and request timestamps) to operate and secure the Services. These logs are automatically rotated, are not used to identify you, and are only reviewed to maintain the reliability and security of the site.

Subdomains and linked resources

This statement covers all subdomains under sounny.github.io. Some tools and pages may link to third-party services (such as external map tiles or fonts). Those services have their own privacy practices; please review their policies when using them.

Your rights

Even though no personal data is intentionally collected, you may contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of any information that could identify you. We will honor valid GDPR and applicable US privacy requests to the extent data exists and can be located.

Data security

Industry-standard security measures are used to protect the Services. Because the site does not store personal data, there is no routine data retention beyond transient technical logs managed by the hosting provider.

Updates

This statement may be updated to reflect changes to the Services or legal requirements. The “Last updated” date below will always indicate the most recent revision.
